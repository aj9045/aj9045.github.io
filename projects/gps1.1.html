OUR FIRST PAIR PROGRAMMING!

AJ & Michael Angelo

What is pair programming?

Two programmers working together on a project.

Why is pair programming important to DBC?

It builds that level of interaction that people are missing these days. It improves interfacing, and skills that you'll be using later as a professional developer.

Compare Driver and Navigator

The driver and navigator are similar, where the driver will take the lead, and the navigator will guide.
